GODZILLA

KING OF THE MONSTERS

6.3 | Action | 2019 | 2 h 12 min

In the sequel to 2014's Godzilla, the crypto-zoological organization Monarch continues to track down ancient creatures they find around the world. However, governments want these ancient creatures to be completely destroyed after the incident in 2014. These ancient super-species, thought to be legends, suddenly awaken again and Godzilla and ancient creatures called titans Mothra, Rodan and King Ghidorah begin to fight to dominate each other. This war will bring humanity to the brink of extinction.
